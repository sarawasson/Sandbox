I have published extensively in critical medical humanities in journals such as the BMJ Medical Humanities, the journal Literature and Medicine hosted at Johns Hopkins University, and my most recent monograph, Transplantation Gothic: Tissue Transfer in Literature, Film and Medicine (Manchester: Manchester University Press, 2020). It was short-listed for the British Society for Literature and Science Award in 2021.

I was Primary Investigator for the UK AHRC-funded interdisciplinary project Translating Chronic Pain (2017–2019) and have developed it into an ongoing international online platform with work from 13 countries and visitors to date from 103 countries.
